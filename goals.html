L-Goals
Life Projects that drive excellence and exploration.

In 2017, I came up with the idea to create a set of goals that would push me out of my comfort zone to obtain a high level of ability and understanding of a select number of specific areas. The areas selected were chosen for a variety of reasons, but mainly to push myself to experience and understand as much of this world and the human experience as I can.
